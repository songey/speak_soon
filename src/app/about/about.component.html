Why ' Speak Soon' ?

'Speak Soon' is free. It connects you to people from around the world who can help you to learn a new language.

What is it ?

Type in any language you want to learn.

This will instantly connect you to people who:

  • .....can help you learn that language!

What's the catch?

Those people are looking to learn your language..

  1. You have to teach them your language
  2. but they will help you to learn theirs...

How exactly does it work?

  1. Type in the language(s) you wish to learn
  2. Type in the language(s) you know

  3. You will be connected to all the other people wishing to learn that language
  4. You will be connected to all the people who speak that language and want to learn your language

Lastly...

What can I do with it?
  • Message people individually or join discussions in the group forum
  • Schedule a time to talk face to face via video.
  • Keep track of your progess in a personal log
  • and much more...
